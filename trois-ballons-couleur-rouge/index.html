LORIC DEMANDER

trois ballons couleur Rouge

Exebition at Schaufenstergalierie SCHARF in Graz, Austria.

18.12.2021 - 13.02.2022

"The space in which we move for now more than almost two years has become very limited. Loric Demander confronts this theme of space both in his photographs and in his film created especially for the "Schaufenstergalerie SCHARF". He takes us on a journey of the diversity of spaces in an unusual way and shows possibilities of different spaces. Space, ambient space, living space: infinity can begin."
