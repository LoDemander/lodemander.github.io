LORIC DEMANDER

Modern Worries

Directed, shot and edited by Loric Demander

"A film that creates its own little cinematic world with speed, precision and humour. This precisely edited and thus feather-light masterpiece lasts only a minute and does without words because it relies entirely on the facial expressions and the physical actions of its main actor. The 16-year-old director of this ultra-short film can also thank his talented cinematographer, his terrific editor and his minimalist sound designer. By the way, they all hide behind one and the same name."

- Video & Filmtage Vienna

"The film is a contemporary document and can give later generations an impression of our reality in April 2020 and the 'Modern Worries' of our time."

- MEDIA LITERACY AWARD

2020
