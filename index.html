Loric Demander is a filmmaker based in Vienna. His work spans advertisements, short films, documentaries, and music videos, which he produces for brands and artists. He also frequently collaborates in the camera, lighting, and production departments of feature films, music videos, and short films.

Active in film and photography since 2015, Loric gained his first on-set experience in 2018 during a workshop with director Jakob M. Erwa. In 2020, he co-wrote and directed the viral satire videos Homestory: Basti and Kochen mit Basti, which were featured on Willkommen Österreich. More recent works have been showcased in several art exhibitions in Graz. Since 2024, he has expanded his portfolio by working on multiple feature films, a TV series, joining the media team at Paleo Festival, and completing a semester at a film school in Canada. In 2025, he began studying cinematography at the Filmakademie Wien.
