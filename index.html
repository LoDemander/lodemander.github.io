Loric Demander is a filmmaker and photographer based in Graz, Austria.

He has been active in film, photography and theatre since 2015. In 2018 he made his first experiences on a professional film set as he participated in the workshop "Film beTEILigt" led by Jakob M. Erwa. The following year, his first short documentary "Aikido" screened at Video&Filmtage in Vienna. In 2020, he created the satire videos „Homestory: Basti“ and „Kochen mit Basti“, in collaboration with Johannes Häfele. The project was featured by the late-night talk show „Willkommen Österreich“ and became known throughout Austria. In the same year, his short film "Modern Worries" was awarded in Vienna at Video&Filmtage and Media Literacy Award. In 2021 he was part of the Diagonale-youth-jury. Later that year, he opened his first exhibition „trois ballons couleur rouge“ at Schaufenstergallerie SCHARF in Graz.

„Irony and wit seem to be in the young filmmaker's blood, so we can't wait to see what else is in store - we're certainly looking forward to more strokes of genius from this all-round talent!“
