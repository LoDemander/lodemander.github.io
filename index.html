About

Loric Demander is a filmmaker and photographer based in Graz, Austria.

Currently a student, he has been active in film, photography and theater since 2015. In 2018 he took over the camera at the workshop "Film beTEILigt" led by Jakob M. Erwa. The following year, his short documentary "Aïkido" screened at the Video&Filmtage in Vienna. In 2020, about Chancellor Kurz, produced in collaboration with Johannes Häfele, became known throughout Austria. In the same year, his short film "Modern Worries" was awarded in Vienna at the Video & Filmtage and the Media Literacy Award. In 2021 he was part of the Diakone 2021 youth jury. Later that year, he opened his expedition „trios ballon cooler rouge“ at Schaufenstergalerie SCHARF.

„Irony and wit seem to be in the young filmmaker's blood, so we can't wait to see what else is in store - we're certainly looking forward to more strokes of genius from this all-round talent!“

Media Literacy Award, 2020
