Loric Demander is an independent filmmaker based in Vienna. His work includes ads, short films, and music videos, which he produces independently for brands and artists. He also frequently collaborates in the camera, lighting, and production departments on feature films, music videos, and short films.

Active in film and photography since 2015, Loric gained his first film set experience in 2018, working with director Jakob M. Erwa. In 2020, he co-wrote and directed the viral satire videos “Homestory: Basti” and “Kochen mit Basti”, which were featured on Willkommen Österreich. His short film “Modern Worries” received recognition at Video&Filmtage and the Media Literacy Award in Vienna the same year. In 2021 he was part of the Diagonale Youth Jury. More recent works have been showcased in multiple art exhibitions in Graz. In 2024, he expanded his portfolio by working on music videos and feature films, joining the media team at Paleo Festival, and completing a semester at a film school in Canada.
